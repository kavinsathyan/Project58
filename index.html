My hobby is drawing. I mostly draw pictures of people, animals and birds. I use crayons, pencils, colour pencils etc to draw pictures. I like to become an artist when I grow up. drawing is a particular hobby which i love it. without colours i can't imagine drawing because without colours the painting looks dull so drawing is the most perfect hobby for me.
